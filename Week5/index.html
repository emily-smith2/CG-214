100,000 Stars Visualization

Many of the Chrome Experiments are enjoyable to look at, this visualization is out of this world educational! By simply zooming in or out with a mouse, users may begin with the sun and learn about over 100,000 nearby stars.

A few elements that I appreciate:

  • Interactive tour
  • Ability to change perspectives
  • Scientifically based
  • Educational, seamlessly connected to Wikipedia

These are the stars closest in our solar system. It would take 18 years for a jet airplane to fly to the sun.

The furthest man made object, Voyager 1 is now almost 17 light hours away. The length of one light year is the distance light can travel in a year.
